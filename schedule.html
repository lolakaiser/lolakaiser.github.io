🥗 Nutrition Goals

100–130g protein daily — non-negotiable for glute growth
Eat more on strength days, a little lighter on Pilates days
Prep lunches Sunday so weekdays are grab-and-go
Hit protein on rest days — that's when muscle is built
Hydrate well — aim for 2–3L water per day
Don't skip breakfast on workout mornings
